Inviting New People


Anyone can invite new people to the Server!

We love new friends, but also want to avoid
toxic people and trolls, we have enough of those.
So in order for the new person to be given the Friend
role and access to the Voice and Text Channels, there
are a couple of steps.

  1. Invite them by right clicking their name or creating a MNG invite
  2. Once they enter, introduce them in #introductions (Or have them introduce themselves but drop your name)
  3. A wingdude should pass them the Friend role upon introduction
  4. They should come read the #rules-and-reminders and hit up #roke-assignment

Full disclosure for this process: We want
recourse if things go poorly, and knowing
connections and friendships is helpful in
having the tougher discussions

Requesting a Game be Added to Reaction Roles

Do you want a game added to the
#role-assignment channel? Do you want to be able
to ping (pester) loads of friends to play
a game with you?

  1. Ensure the game is already being played by a few people on the server.
    If it's not being played yet: It's your job to promote it and take the lead on getting people involved!
    If a game is being played, then we can create a role for it:

  2. Provide a Wing Dude the following details:
    Name of the Game
    Category # on #role-assignment (1-5)
    Emoji. (Pick one that isn't already being used, stock emoji's preferred)

Requesting a Game for Monday Night

  1. Please bring it to us before Monday!

  2. Give people a heads up so they can buy
    it, get it downloaded, play the tutorial,
    whatever needs to happen to make game go vroom.



Otherwise it won't get voted for and played and/or
we'll all sit around on game night waiting for a download
or listening to each other play the tutorial

Getting Games Going on Monday Night

Please at least come say hello in the game night voice chat, night of!!!

But if you want to play something and other people voted for it, then tell them and help the process along!

We aren't mind readers and there's only so many times I can suggest trying a different game once things get going. Hit us with that clue by four.

Please, don't be shy!!! The infrastructure is set like this for a reason, we will get you set up in a Voice Chat (Yes, even if we need to make a new room!)
