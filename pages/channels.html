INTRODUCING FRIENDS

#introductions - A public channel for introducing yourself (or the friends you invite if you are reading this), letting us know who invited.

Anyone can invite new people to the Server!

They should come read the #rules-and-reminders-ðŸ¤º and hit up #role-assignment

In order for the new person to be given the Friend role and access to the Voice and Text Channels, there are a couple of steps.

  1. Invite them by right clicking their name or creating a MNG invite.
  2. Once they enter, introduce them in #introductions (Or have them introduce themselves but drop your name).
  3. A Wingdude should pass them the Friend role upon introduction. When granted the @Friends role, that will unlock more channels.
