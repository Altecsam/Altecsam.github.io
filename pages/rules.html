Rules

Come as friends, stay as family

The founding principle of Monday Night Games (MNG) is for like minded gamers to share a good time. Everyone here should be aware of participant expectations:

  • Bring a positive attitude
  • Be adaptable
  • Be mindful of other people (i.e. What's offensive for some may not be for others, if you upset someone apologize!)
  • This is a collective, please contribute your ideas!
  • Check yourself out if necessary, take it to Toxic Dump

Monday Nights 7:30pm EST until (usually) 11:00p EST

RULE 1

There is no losing at Game Night

We play a variety of games, some are cooperative some are not.
The point is to hang out, be silly, and play with a bunch of people we like. We can schedule TRY HARD mode another night.
If you find yourself getting tilted try to remember the point is not winning, but having fun.
You are responsible for checking yourself out if you need to take a break. There is always another game or game mode right around the corner.
Monday Nights typically last 4 hours so there’s plenty of time... and we’ll be back next week.

TL;DR - Be present and positive. If you can’t, come back next week. (As Stige would say: Bye Felicia! For now.)

RULE 2

Be Constructive and Contribute

If you find you don’t like a game or game mode, suggest another one for the next round (or next Monday)! Please don’t complain,
it makes it a lot less fun to play and a lot less fun hanging out with you.
You can always take the lead! This is a collective of gamers, not a game-tatorship, contrary to Sam's dreams.
If you really hate a game we play or want a different one, We are open to suggestions!
We have 10-15 players typically and it takes time for everyone to buy a game, but we are ALWAYS open to try new things.
Put it in LFG and hype it up... once people own it and are playing, we are so happy to add it to the vote pool for Mondays.

TL;DR - Contribute more than hot air and criticism

RULE 3

Harassment will not be tolerated

Be kind to one another. We do like to joke around and there’s definitely some trash talk, but it needs to come from a place of friendship.
If someone is getting uncomfortable, please stop.
If you are uncomfortable it is up to you to let us know. Please tell the person making you uncomfortable, if you are really uncomfortable doing that come talk to me.
Respect your teammates and opponents! This one cannot be emphasized enough. Remember that when you are playing we are all friends here. Flaming, trolling,
targeted blaming and poor sportmanship targeted at teammates and opponents will not be tolerated. Sometimes a little trolling is acceptable, but if someone asks
you to cut it out, stop.

TL;DR - Don't be a dick
